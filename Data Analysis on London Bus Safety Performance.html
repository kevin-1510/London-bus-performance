Problem Statement

There were many Bus operators operating in the City of London between the year 2015 to 2018. Many operators were involved in unfortunate incidents, which makes the performance of these operators questionable among the commuters.

Objective

The objective of this project is to find out which operator performed well (i.e) which involved in least number of incidents.

Analysis

From the analysis we have done, we found out that “TGM Group” is the safest operator and “Slip Trip Fall” is the most common incident happened in most of the buses. It is advised that foot board travel should not be encouraged, the foot board of the buses be re-engineered, and boarding a running bus should not be encouraged.
